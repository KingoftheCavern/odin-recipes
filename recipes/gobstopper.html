Everlasting Gobstopper

Description:

The Everlasting Gobstopper is a never-ending cascade of flavor. You can suck on it forever and it'll never shrink or lose flavor! It's a truly marvelous invention, courtesy of Willy Wonka.

Ingredients:

  • A small porous rock (such as pumice)
  • High fructose corn syrup (1 liter)
  • Food coloring of your choice (200ml)
  • Beeswax (20 grams)

Steps:

  1. Chisel rock into desired shape
  2. Dunk rock into corn syrup
  3. Remove and apply layer of food coloring
  4. Apply layer of melted beeswax
  5. Cool until beeswax is solid
  6. Repeat steps 2 through 5 1000 times or until out of materials
  7. Do not bite!

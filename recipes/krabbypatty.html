Krabby Patty

Description:

The Krabby Patty is Eugene Krabs' finest creation. The recipe for this delicious patty is a trade secret, but by going to your local grocery store's freezer section, you can create one in your own kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Krabby Patty box (1 patty)

Steps:

  1. Remove 1 Krabby Patty from package and put onto microwave safe plate
  2. Nuke patty for 1 minute and 10 seconds
  3. Enjoy (if possible)!
